Jackson Holliday

Jackson Holliday is a highly touted left-handed hitting infielder for the Baltimore Orioles, drafted 1st overall in 2022 out of Stillwater High School (OK). The son of seven-time MLB All-Star Matt Holliday, Jackson broke the national high school record with 89 hits in a single season. He made his MLB debut in 2024 at age 20 and is known for his rapid ascent through the minors and elite pedigree
