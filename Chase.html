Ja'Marr Chase

Ja'Marr Chase (born March 1, 2000) is a star NFL wide receiver for the Cincinnati Bengals, drafted 5th overall in 2021 out of LSU. Known for elite speed and physicality, he is a 4-time Pro Bowler (2021-2024), 2021 Offensive Rookie of the Year, and set NFL records for receiving yards by a rookie in the Super Bowl era.
