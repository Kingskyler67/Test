Kenyon Sadiq

Kenyon Sadiq (born March 4, 2005) is an elite Oregon Ducks tight end (Class of 2023) known as a "freak athlete" for his speed. As of April 2026, he is a projected high-round 2026 NFL Draft pick, measuring 6'3", 245 lbs, who set Oregon single-season records for tight end receptions (51) and 40-yard dash times (4.39s).
