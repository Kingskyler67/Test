(JSN) Jaxon Smith Nijgba

Jaxon Smith-Njigba ("JSN"), born February 14, 2002, is a star wide receiver for the Seattle Seahawks, drafted 20th overall in 2023. Following a record-setting college career at Ohio State, JSN broke the Seahawks' single-season receiving record in 2025 with 1,309 yards. Known for elite route running and a 4.48-second 40-yard dash, he is an emerging NFL superstar and 2025 Offensive Player of the Year.
