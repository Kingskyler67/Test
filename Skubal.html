Terik Skubal

Tarik Skubal is a dominant left-handed pitcher for the Detroit Tigers, established as one of MLB's premier aces by winning consecutive American League Cy Young Awards in 2024 and 2025. A 2024 All-Star and Triple Crown winner, he is known for a high-velocity fastball reaching over 100 mph, elite strikeout numbers, and exceptional control, winning 18 games in 2024.
