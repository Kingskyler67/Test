Ryan Williams

Ryan Williams is a standout Alabama Crimson Tide wide receiver born on February 9, 2007, known for reclassifying from the 2025 to the 2024 recruiting class as a five-star prospect. Nicknamed "Hollywood," he is the first two-time Alabama Mr. Football winner and gained national attention as a true freshman in 2024 for his elite playmaking ability and scoring over 1,300 yards in high schoo.
