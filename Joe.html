Joe Burrow

Joe Burrow is a star NFL quarterback for the Cincinnati Bengals, drafted first overall in 2020 after a historic 2019 Heisman-winning season at LSU. Known as "Joe Cool" or "Joe Brrr," he led the Bengals to Super Bowl LVI in his second year, earning 2x NFL Comeback Player of the Year (2021, 2024).
