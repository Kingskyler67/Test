Why Gunnar stands out

Gunnar Henderson (born June 29, 2001) is a standout Baltimore Orioles infielder who won the 2023 American League Rookie of the Year and a Silver Slugger award. A standout shortstop/third baseman known for power, speed, and a strong arm, he was drafted in 2019 out of Alabama and made his MLB debut in 2022.
