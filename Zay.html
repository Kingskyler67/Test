Zay Flowers

Xavien "Zay" Flowers (born September 11, 2000) is a standout Baltimore Ravens wide receiver drafted 22nd overall in 2023 out of Boston College. Known for his speed and agility, the 5'9", 183 lb Florida native broke all major Boston College receiving records before becoming a key offensive weapon for Baltimore, exceeding 800+ yards in his debut NFL season.
