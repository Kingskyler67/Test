Jeramiah Jackson

Jeremiah Jackson (born March 26, 2000) is a professional baseball infielder/outfielder for the Baltimore Orioles organization, drafted in the second round of the 2018 MLB Draft by the Angels. Known for his power, he tied the Pioneer League single-season home run record (23) in 2019. He made his MLB debut for the Orioles on August 1, 2025
