Nikola Jokic

Nikola Jokić (born February 19, 1995) is a Serbian professional basketball player for the Denver Nuggets, widely regarded as one of the greatest centers and NBA draft steals of all time. Nicknamed "Joker," the 6'11" center has won three NBA MVPs (2021, 2022, 2024), the 2023 NBA Championship, and 2023 Finals MVP.
