Fernado Mendoza

Fernando Gabriel Mendoza V (born October 1, 2003) is an American football quarterback who won the 2025 Heisman Trophy and led the Indiana Hoosiers to their first national championship in 2025 (played in Jan. 2026) after transferring from Cal. The former 2-star recruit and Miami native is projected as a top 2026 NFL draft pick.
