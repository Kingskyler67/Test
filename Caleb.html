Caleb Downs

Caleb Downs is an elite safety selected 11th overall by the Dallas Cowboys in the 2026 NFL Draft after a standout college career at Alabama (2023) and Ohio State (2024–2025). A 2025 Thorpe and Lott IMPACT Trophy winner, he recorded 256 career tackles, 6 interceptions, and 2 touchdowns. Known for exceptional instincts, he was a unanimous All-American in 2024 and 2025.
