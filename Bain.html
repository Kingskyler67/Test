Rueban Bain Jr

Rueben Bain Jr. (born September 8, 2004) is a highly disruptive edge rusher from Miami, Florida, who was selected by the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in the 2026 NFL Draft. As a standout at the University of Miami, he was a 2025 Consensus All-American, the ACC Defensive Player of the Year, and winner of the Ted Hendricks Award as the nation's top defensive end.
