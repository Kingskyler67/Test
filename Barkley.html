Saquan Barkley

Saquon Barkley is a star NFL running back known for his explosive playstyle, drafted 2nd overall by the NY Giants in 2018 before joining the Philadelphia Eagles in 2024. A Penn State legend, he won NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year (2018), is a multiple-time Pro Bowler, and is known for his immense strength, including a renowned 400+ lb power clean.
