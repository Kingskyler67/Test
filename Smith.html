Jerimiah Smith

Jeremiah Smith is a standout Ohio State Buckeyes wide receiver and the No. 1 overall recruit in the 2024 class. As a true freshman, he became a key playmaker, earning 2025 Rose Bowl Offensive MVP honors with a massive 1,300+ yard season. Known for his elite 6'3", 215-lb frame, he is already considered one of college football's top receivers.
