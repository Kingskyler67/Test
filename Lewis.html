Ray Lewis

Ray Lewis is a Hall of Fame linebacker who played his entire 17-year NFL career (1996–2012) with the Baltimore Ravens, winning two Super Bowls (XXXV, XLVII) and two Defensive Player of the Year awards (2000, 2003). Widely considered one of the greatest middle linebackers in NFL history, he recorded over 2,000 career tackles and is the only player in NFL history with 40+ sacks and 30+ interceptions.
