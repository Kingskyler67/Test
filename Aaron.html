Aaron Judge

Aaron Judge (born April 26, 1992) is a 6'7", 282-pound American League (AL) MVP right fielder for the New York Yankees. He set the AL record for home runs in a single season (62 in 2022) and is known for his immense power, holding 8 20-home run seasons. He is a multi-time All-Star, 2017 Rookie of the Year, and signed a 9-year, $360M contract in 2022
